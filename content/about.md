## Who I Am

I'm Harsh Jha — an AI Engineer who believes that the most powerful technology
disappears into simplicity. I build systems that think, learn, and create,
but I measure their worth by how naturally they serve the people who use them.

## What I Do

I work at the intersection of **machine learning**, **software engineering**,
and **product thinking**. My days involve:

- Designing and deploying **LLM-powered applications** and autonomous agents
- Building **data pipelines** and **ML infrastructure** that scale
- Architecting systems where AI components integrate cleanly with production code
- Exploring **multi-agent orchestration** and **reasoning frameworks**

## Philosophy

> "Shibui (渋い) — simple, subtle, and unobtrusive beauty."

I believe the best engineering, like the best design, is invisible.
Clean abstractions over clever hacks. Readable code over brilliant code.
Systems that do one thing extraordinarily well.
